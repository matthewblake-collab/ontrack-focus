## Data Quality Issues

1. **Duplicate entry:** Magnesium Glycinate appears twice with slightly different dosage text and benefits
2. **LL-37 dosage:** Our 1-5mg is potentially 10-50x higher than peptide-db's 100-150mcg injectable recommendation - needs urgent review
3. **SS-31:** We say "dosing under investigation" but peptide-db provides specific protocols used in clinical trials - our entry is overly conservative
4. **FOXO4-DRI:** We say "not established" but peptide-db extrapolates from mouse studies (5mg/kg) to human dosing (25-33mg)
5. **Epitalon:** Dosage format inconsistency - we describe total cycle dose, peptide-db describes per-injection daily dose
6. **Glutathione:** We only describe oral supplementation; peptide-db covers injectable (SubQ/IV) protocols which are the peptide-relevant route

---

## Recommendations

1. **Fix LL-37 dosage immediately** - potential safety concern if users interpret our 1-5mg as standard
2. **Update SS-31 and FOXO4-DRI** with available dosing data
3. **Remove duplicate Magnesium Glycinate** entry
4. **Add missing benefits** from the High Priority table above
5. **Consider adding MK-677, Humanin, L-Carnitine, and Rapamycin** as high-relevance missing compounds
6. **Add injectable Glutathione** information alongside oral
7. **Standardise dosage format** - some entries mix total cycle vs per-dose descriptions
