# OnTrack Focus — Social Media Content Plan

**Platforms:** Instagram · Facebook · TikTok  
**Username:** @ontrack_focus  
**Stage:** TestFlight live → App Store launch  
**Constraint:** Solo founder, under 3 hours/week  
**Last updated:** April 2026

---

## 1. Content Pillars

Five themes. Every post lives in one of them. This keeps the account focused and makes batching fast.

### Pillar 1 — Accountability & Showing Up (30%)
The emotional core of the product. Group accountability, streaks, not quitting when it gets hard. Speaks directly to both audiences — people struggling to stay consistent AND people ready to start. This is the "why" behind the app.

Topics: streak milestones, group challenges, showing up on bad days, the moment you stop making excuses, what real accountability looks like, why willpower isn't enough.

### Pillar 2 — Founder Story & Building in Public (25%)
Matt's personal journey is the #1 differentiator. Ex-rugby league player, 80-hour weeks, lost his way, rebuilt with two mates, owned a gym, built OnTrack. No other app has this story. Use it. Building in public = shipping new features + sharing honest behind-the-scenes from Port Macquarie.

Topics: the rugby league story, working away from family, what got Matt back, why he built the app, decisions made, features rejected, what solo founder life looks like.

### Pillar 3 — Feature Drops & App Updates (20%)
Every new build = a content moment. Short demo videos (screen record or Remotion-generated), UI reveals, "what just shipped" posts. This drives TestFlight downloads and App Store hype. Fast to produce, high trust signal.

Topics: new feature walkthroughs, before/after UI improvements, "why we built this" explainers, build changelogs in human language.

### Pillar 4 — Wellness & Consistency Education (15%)
Value-first content that attracts the audience before they know about the app. Tips on habits, supplements, sleep, recovery, mental health. Positions OnTrack as the authority, not just the app.

Topics: habit stacking, supplement timing, sleep tracking, why streaks work, how to build a morning routine, what consistency actually looks like, AI wellness insights.

### Pillar 5 — Community & Social Proof (10%)
TestFlight tester shoutouts, group milestones, community wins. Builds the tribe and creates FOMO for people not yet in the app.

Topics: tester stories, screenshots of streaks, group session check-ins, quote/testimonials from real users, "look what this group is doing."

## 3. Repeatable Weekly Content Workflow (~2.5 hours)

Do this same routine every week. Batching is the only way this works solo.

### Monday — Plan & Write (45 min)
- Pick the week's 3 Reel topics (one per pillar: Accountability, Founder, Feature)
- Write 3 captions in full
- Write 2 Story prompts (poll, question, or quick tip)
- Note any community wins or tester shoutouts from the week

### Tuesday — Record (60 min)
- Record 3 Reels back-to-back (phone, no studio needed)
- Keep it raw and real — that's the brand
- Record in one location, batch outfits if needed
- For feature Reels: use screen record on iPhone or Remotion-generated preview

### Wednesday — Edit & Schedule (45 min)
- Add captions/subtitles to all videos (CapCut is fastest)
- Upload to Instagram + TikTok natively (no watermark)
- Schedule Facebook reposts
- Queue Stories for Thu–Sun

### Daily — Engage (10 min/day)
- Reply to all comments on recent posts
- Comment on 3–5 posts in niche (habit tracking, fitness, wellness accounts)
- No DMs required until community grows

**Total: ~2.5 hours/week**

## 4. Per-Build Content Workflow

Every time a new feature ships, follow this exact sequence. New builds are a content asset — treat them like a mini launch.

### Step 1 — Capture (10 min, same day as build)
- Screen record a quick walkthrough of the new feature on device
- Take 2–3 screenshots of the new UI
- Optional: Run Remotion to generate a polished feature preview video

### Step 2 — Write the "What Just Shipped" post (10 min)
Use this template:
```
Just shipped: [feature name].

[One sentence on what it does.]
[One sentence on why it matters / what problem it solves.]
[One sentence on what's next or a small honest detail about building it.]

OnTrack is free on TestFlight → link in bio.
```

### Step 3 — Post across platforms (10 min)
- Instagram Reel: screen record walkthrough + caption
- Instagram Story: swipe-through screenshots + "What's new" sticker
- TikTok: same Reel, no watermark
- Facebook: repurposed Reel or screenshot post

### Step 4 — Archive for App Store prep
Save every feature video and caption to a `build-content/` folder. These become the App Store preview video, screenshots, and release notes when launch time comes.

**Total per build: ~30 min**

## 9. Content Rules (Non-Negotiables)

1. **Always write in Matt's voice** — first person, direct, personal. Never "the app" or "the team."
2. **Never preachy** — don't tell people what they should be doing. Stand beside them.
3. **Real beats polished** — a talking-head Reel from the car beats a studio-produced promo.
4. **Every post has one job** — either drive to TestFlight/App Store OR build trust. Not both at once.
5. **Consistency over virality** — show up every week. The algorithm rewards regularity more than one big post.
6. **No watermarks on TikTok** — always native-upload or use SnapTik to strip IG watermarks.
