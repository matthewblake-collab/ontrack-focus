# OnTrack Focus — Product Marketing Context

## Product
- **Name:** OnTrack Focus
- **Platform:** iOS (SwiftUI)
- **Stage:** Live on TestFlight, approaching App Store submission
- **Developer:** Solo founder, Port Macquarie, NSW, Australia
- **Website:** ontrack-focus.com
- **TestFlight:** https://testflight.apple.com/join/q65zPgbv

## What It Is
OnTrack Focus is a wellness and group accountability app for iOS. It helps users build habits, track supplements, log daily check-ins, and stay accountable through group sessions and a social friends layer.

## Core Features
- Daily habit tracking with streaks and group accountability
- Group sessions — create, RSVP, attend, track streaks together
- Supplement stack tracker with stock management and reminders
- Daily check-in (sleep, energy, wellbeing scored 1–10) with AI wellness insights
- Friends tab — social feed, friend codes, activity feed, likes
- Mental health trends with Swift Charts
- HealthKit integration
- Push notifications and smart reminders

## Target Audience
- People who are already putting in the work but struggling to stay consistent
- People who are ready for a real change and looking for the right system to start
- Gym-goers and fitness-focused people who train in groups
- Biohackers and supplement users who want to stay on track
- Anyone who knows accountability is what's been missing from their routine

## Positioning
Group accountability meets personal wellness tracking. Not just a habit tracker — a social layer that makes you actually show up. Built for people who are serious about their goals and for people who are finally ready to start showing up for them.

## Tone of Voice
Motivating, direct, no fluff. Confident without being aggressive. Speaks to two types of people equally — those already in the grind who need the right system around them, and those ready to make a real change and looking for somewhere to start. Never preachy. Always in their corner.

## Competitors
Habitica, Streaks, HabitNow, generic habit trackers. OnTrack differentiates through the combination of group accountability + supplement tracking + mental health check-ins in one app.

## Primary CTA
Download on TestFlight (moving to App Store soon)

## Business Model
Free during TestFlight. Monetisation TBD — likely freemium with premium features unlocked via subscription.

## Key Messages
- "Show up for your goals — together"
- Accountability is the missing ingredient in most wellness routines
- Built for people who are serious and for people who are ready to be
- One app for habits, supplements, sessions, and mental health
- Your group keeps you honest. Your data keeps you motivated.

## Founder Story

### Short (website hero / tagline)
"I was a professional rugby league player who couldn't keep himself healthy working 80-hour weeks away from his family. If I couldn't stay consistent, anyone can lose their way. OnTrack is what I wish I'd had."

### Medium (about section / App Store description)
"I played professional rugby league. I had the talent. What I didn't have was the right system around me — the consistency, the mindset, the accountability. Years later, working 70-80 hour weeks away from my young family, I'd let it all slip. I wasn't looking after myself and I knew it.

The thing that changed everything wasn't a program or an app. It was two mates who were in the same spot. We made it competitive, we kept each other honest, and we all came out the other side better for it.

I owned a gym after that. Loved the community more than anything. But the travel made it impossible to stay connected to the people I actually wanted to help. So I built OnTrack. Not because I had it all figured out. Because I've been exactly where you are, and I know what actually works. A group of people who won't let you quit."

### Long (founder story page / Instagram / launch post)
"I played professional rugby league. I had the talent but never quite made it to the top level — and looking back now I know exactly why. I didn't have the right system around me. The consistency, the mindset, the mental edge that separates good from great. When my playing days ended, so did the structure that had been holding everything together.

A few years later I was working 70-80 hour weeks away from home, away from my young family, not looking after myself and not even pretending to. The thing I had to learn the hard way is that you can't do it for anyone else first. Not your partner, not your kids. You have to want it for yourself. That's the secret to habits and consistency that nobody tells you.

What got me back wasn't willpower or a program. It was two mates who were in exactly the same spot. We joined a fitness challenge together, made it competitive, checked in on each other, and pushed each other along — not because we were trying to be someone's coach, but because none of us wanted to be the one who quit. That's when I understood what actually drives change. It's not information or motivation. It's the people around you.

I went on to own a gym and loved every second of the community side of it. But the travel meant I couldn't be there day to day, and that daily connection was the whole point. So I moved home to Port Macquarie and started helping the people around me the same way those two mates helped me. Unapologetically but with care. Holding them accountable, celebrating the wins, not letting them disappear when it gets hard.

OnTrack is that scaled. It's the group that won't let you quit, the system that shows you how far you've come, and the app I wish existed when I was working those 80-hour weeks wondering why I couldn't get out of my own way. If you've been where I've been — or you're there right now — this is for you."

— Matt, Founder of OnTrack Focus
