# Viral Content Patterns — OnTrack Focus

8 short-form video archetypes that work for app/self-improvement content. Every piece of content should use one of these structures.

---

## 1. The Impossible Demo

**Pattern:** Show a result that looks too fast or too easy. Viewer thinks "wait, how?" and keeps watching.

**OnTrack example:**
- "Full supplement stack set up in 30 seconds" — screen record at normal speed
- "One tap and my whole day is tracked" — show Daily Actions view completing
- "My group can see my streak in real time" — show the friend feed updating

**Key:** Must be real speed. Don't fake it. The app being genuinely fast IS the content.

**Best platform:** TikTok, Instagram Reels

---

## 2. The Myth Bust

**Pattern:** Call out bad advice everyone follows. State the myth, pause, then destroy it with specifics.

**OnTrack example:**
- Myth: "You need 21 days to build a habit" → Reality: research says 66 days average, and it varies wildly by habit type
- Myth: "Just use Apple Health to track everything" → Reality: it tracks data, not behaviour — no streaks, no accountability, no social layer
- Myth: "Motivation is what you need" → Reality: systems beat motivation. Your group beats your willpower.

**Key:** The bust must be specific, not just "that's wrong." Give the real answer.

**Best platform:** All platforms

---

## 3. The Before/After Data

**Pattern:** Show real tracking data that tells a story. Not body transformation — behaviour transformation.

**OnTrack example:**
- Week 1 streak vs Week 12 streak — screenshot comparison
- Supplement adherence: 40% in month 1 → 92% in month 3
- Daily check-in data showing sleep improvement over time
- "My first week on OnTrack vs my 90th day" — side by side screens

**Key:** Real data only. Never fabricate. The authenticity IS the value.

**Best platform:** Instagram (carousel), TikTok

---

## 4. The Speed Setup

**Pattern:** Set up a complete feature from scratch in under 60 seconds. Makes the app look easy and powerful.

**OnTrack example:**
- "Build your full morning routine in OnTrack in 45 seconds" — add 3 habits, set times, done
- "Set up your supplement protocol in 60 seconds" — add from Knowledge Library, set reminders
- "Create a training group with your mates in 30 seconds" — create group, share invite code

**Key:** No narration explaining what you're doing — let the screen speak. Add text overlay for key moments only.

**Best platform:** TikTok, Instagram Reels

---

## 5. The Comparison

**Pattern:** Side-by-side with a competitor or the "old way." Not trash talk — genuine differences shown visually.

**OnTrack example:**
- OnTrack vs Apple Health: "One tracks data. One tracks behaviour."
- OnTrack vs Streaks: "Streaks tracks habits. OnTrack tracks habits AND whether your group showed up."
- OnTrack vs a Notes app: "This is how most people 'track' their supplements" → show messy notes → show OnTrack protocol view

**Key:** Be factual. Show real screens. Let the viewer draw the conclusion. Never say "we're better" — show it.

**Best platform:** Instagram Reels, TikTok

---

## 6. The "Why You're Failing"

**Pattern:** Diagnose a common problem the viewer has experienced. Make them feel seen, then offer the fix.

**OnTrack example:**
- "You're not lazy. You just don't have anyone watching." → group accountability pitch
- "You quit supplements because there's no system" → show protocol view
- "Tracking in your head doesn't count" → show the difference between mental tracking and actual data
- "Your fitness app doesn't have a social feed. That's why you stopped opening it."

**Key:** Never be condescending. The tone is "I've been there too" not "here's what you're doing wrong."

**Best platform:** All platforms (highest engagement pattern overall)

---

## 7. The Feature Drop

**Pattern:** Show one specific feature people didn't know existed. Fast, focused, no fluff.

**OnTrack example:**
- "Did you know OnTrack auto-fills your check-in from Apple Health?"
- "The Knowledge Library has 70+ supplement protocols with dosing guides"
- "Your group can see each other's streaks in real time"
- "One tap to log every supplement in your morning stack"

**Key:** One feature per video. Don't cram three features in. Show it working within 3 seconds.

**Best platform:** Instagram Reels, TikTok

---

## 8. The User Win

**Pattern:** Real result from a real user. Screenshot, quote, or video testimonial.

**OnTrack example:**
- Early user hits a 30-day streak — screenshot with their permission
- "One of our testers hasn't missed a session in 6 weeks" — real data shown
- Matt's own tracking data — founder using his own app
- Group milestone: "This group has a combined 200-day streak"

**Key:** Must be real. Ask permission. Use anonymised screenshots if needed. The authenticity is non-negotiable.

**Best platform:** Instagram (Stories + Reels), TikTok

---

## Pattern Selection Guide

| Goal | Best Patterns |
|------|--------------|
| Grow followers (MACRO) | Myth Bust, Why You're Failing, Data Hooks |
| Drive downloads (MICRO) | Speed Setup, Comparison, Feature Drop |
| Build trust (BOTH) | User Win, Before/After Data, Impossible Demo |
| Founder brand | Myth Bust + personal angle, Why You're Failing + "I've been there" |
